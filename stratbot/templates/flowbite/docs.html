The trigger and/or target price do not match my chart. Why?

We do not currently have full market coverage (it's incredibly expensive), so OHLC values on intraday timeframes may be off by a few cents compared to TradingView, thinkorswim, and other brokers. Setups on the 15, 30, 60, 4H, and D timeframes are re-processed at 12:15am EST each day and include full market coverage so OHLC values should match your platform.
